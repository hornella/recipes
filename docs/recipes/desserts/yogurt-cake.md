# Healthy Yogurt Cake

A light, moist yogurt cake made with Greek yogurt and maple syrup. Lower in sugar and fat than traditional cakes while still fluffy and satisfying.

## Servings
8 slices

## Ingredients

- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt (0–2%)
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tbsp neutral oil (canola or avocado)
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Optional Add-ins

- 1/2 cup blueberries or mixed berries
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F / 175°C**.
2. Lightly grease or line a **9-inch loaf pan or small cake pan**.
3. In a large bowl, whisk together:
   - Greek yogurt  
   - eggs  
   - maple syrup  
   - vanilla extract  
   - oil
4. In another bowl, mix:
   - flour  
   - baking powder  
   - salt
5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
6. Fold in optional add-ins if using.
7. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
8. Bake for **35–40 minutes**, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
9. Let cool in the pan for **10–15 minutes**, then transfer to a plate to finish cooling.

## Notes

- For a **lemon yogurt cake**, add lemon zest and 1 tbsp lemon juice.
- For extra protein, replace **1/4 cup flour with vanilla protein powder**.
- This cake keeps well **3–4 days in the fridge**.

## Approximate Nutrition (per slice)

- Calories: ~150  
- Protein: ~6–7 g  
- Fat: ~4 g
