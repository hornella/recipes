# Classic Homemade Sandwich Bread (1 Loaf)

## Yield
- 1 standard loaf (9x5 inch pan)
- Approximately 12 slices

---

## Ingredients
- 3 cups (360 g) bread flour
- 1⅓ cups (315 ml) warm water (40–43°C / 105–110°F)
- 1¼ tsp active dry yeast
- 2 tbsp canola oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp bread booster (optional)

---

## Instructions

1. **Activate Yeast**  
   In a large bowl, mix warm water and sugar. Add yeast and let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

2. **Mix Dough**  
   Add oil, salt, bread booster (if using), and flour.  
   Mix until a soft dough forms. Adjust with 1–2 tbsp water only if dough feels dry.

3. **Knead**  
   Knead for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.  
   Dough should be soft but not sticky.

4. **First Rise**  
   Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl. Cover and let rise 60–75 minutes, until doubled in size.

5. **Shape**  
   Gently punch down. Shape into a tight log and place in a greased 9x5 loaf pan.

6. **Second Rise**  
   Let rise 30–45 minutes, until dough reaches about 1 inch above the rim.

7. **Bake**  
   Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 35–40 minutes, until golden brown and the bottom sounds hollow when tapped.

8. **Cool Properly**  
   Remove from pan after 3–5 minutes.  
   Cool completely on its side or on a rack before slicing.

---

## Storage

- Slice and freeze in airtight bags.
- Toast directly from frozen.
