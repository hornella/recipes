# Crispy Air Fryer Fries

## Description
Easy homemade air fryer fries that are crispy outside and soft inside with minimal oil. A lighter alternative to deep-fried fries.

---

## Servings
2 servings

---

## Ingredients
- 2 medium russet potatoes (about 450–500 g total)
- 1 tbsp olive oil *(or 2 tsp for a lower calorie version)*
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp paprika *(optional)*
- ¼ tsp garlic powder *(optional)*
- Black pepper to taste

---

## Instructions

1. **Cut the potatoes**
   - Peel if desired.
   - Slice into even sticks about 1 cm thick.

2. **Soak for crispiness**
   - Place cut potatoes in cold water.
   - Soak for **20–30 minutes** to remove excess starch.

3. **Dry thoroughly**
   - Drain potatoes.
   - Pat completely dry with a clean towel.

4. **Season**
   - Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, paprika, garlic powder, and black pepper.

5. **Air fry**
   - Preheat air fryer to **190°C / 375°F**.
   - Cook for **15–20 minutes**, shaking the basket halfway through.
   - For extra crispiness, increase temperature to **200°C / 400°F** for the final **3–5 minutes**.

---

## Notes
- Do not overcrowd the air fryer basket; cook in batches if necessary.
- Adding **1 tsp cornstarch** before oil can make the fries extra crispy.
- Salting after cooking can improve crisp texture.

---

## Nutrition (approximate)
**Per recipe (2 servings):**
- Potatoes: ~350 kcal  
- Olive oil (1 tbsp): ~120 kcal  

**Total:** ~470 kcal  
**Per serving:** ~235 kcal

*Using 2 tsp oil instead reduces the total to about ~430 kcal (~215 kcal per serving).*
